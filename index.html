2. OpEx (Operational Costs)

OpEx includes recurring costs like power, staff, cooling, hardware refresh cycles, and software licensing.
CategoryRange / Unit

Support Software LicensesVaries (open source or vendor support)

Estimated Annual Total: $1.5M - $2.8M (for 1MW)

Power Cost Calculation: 1 MW = 1000 kW. Annual usage = 1000 kW × 24 hours × 365 days = 8,760,000 kWh. At $0.10–$0.15 per kWh, annual cost = **$876,000 – $1,314,000**.

5. Recommendations

Practical steps to optimize costs and maximize value in AI infrastructure.
  • Shared GPU infrastructure can reduce costs by 30–50%.
  • Use Slurm for job scheduling and containerized workloads.
  • Leverage grants and vendor credits (AWS, NVIDIA, Dell) for startup costs.

6. Risks & Sustainability Considerations

Addressing potential risks and ensuring sustainable AI data center operations.
  • Energy Costs: Mitigated with renewable energy credits and efficient cooling.
  • Vendor Lock-In: Use open orchestration and container standards.
  • Long-Term Viability: GPU reuse and hybrid cloud strategies.
